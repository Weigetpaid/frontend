A small list of useful resources for your convenience

Contact Emergency Services

Contacting emergency services should be the first move when faced with a dire situation that proves life threatening or could course serious damage to property. Emergency services include :

  • Ambulance
  • Fire Services
  • Police

Natural Disasters

Natural disasters are an inescapable factor when living in Australia. Being well prepared and having a backup plan for evacuation is crucial to ensure the safety of you and your family.

Brisbane City Council Disaster Plan

Brisbane City Council provides a wide range of resources to help prepare for any form of disaster. This includes being able to receive early warning signs and detailed guides on how to prepare your property for crisis.

BOM Early Warning System

The Bureau of Meteorology provides severe weather warning conditions if dangerous weather is expected in an area or will soon expand into an area. See their website for more information on how warnings are handled.
